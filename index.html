Feature I — The Artist

About
her.

Caitlin is a Los Angeles-based actor and voice actor at the start of her career — and all the hungrier for it. Her specialty is colorful, characterful voiceover: the mischievous sidekick, the over-caffeinated narrator, the villain who's having entirely too much fun.

She trains in voiceover and on-camera acting with David Babich in Burbank and Hollywood, where the weekly scene and booth work keeps the instrument warm and the range growing — from commercial reads to animation, games, and narration.

Between classes she reads biography, drinks coffee black, and keeps a running list of words she wants to say out loud one day. Petrichor was a recent favorite.
